# Economics

## Problem

Many users subscribe to multiple AI tools without tracking overlap, inactive subscriptions, or overall spending efficiency.

AIStack Audit aims to help users identify unnecessary AI spending and optimize subscription costs.

---

# Business Opportunity

The rapid growth of AI SaaS products has created a new category of subscription fatigue.

Users often:
- Pay for overlapping tools
- Forget inactive subscriptions
- Lack centralized visibility into AI spending

This creates demand for AI cost optimization platforms.
